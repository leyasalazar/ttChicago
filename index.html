CONECTA

Conecta is a social platform for women and nonbinary to find mentors and virtual chats connecting users with common interests and objectives.

Learn More

ABOUT

Our mission is to provide a safe and empowering space for gender minorities in technology to network, regardless of experience. We strive to encourage networking and find a mentor to pursue technology and computing with more tools and support.

We facilitate casual conversations that lead to not-so-casual professional impact. You are going to find a wide community who can support you in your professional journey.
